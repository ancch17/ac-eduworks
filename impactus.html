Our Philosophy

Learning Built Around People, Not Programmes

At Impactus Learning, we believe that transformation starts with people — not slide decks. Every session is designed to connect learners with the tools, mindsets, and confidence they need to lead in an AI-powered world.

Led by Dr. Andrew Chew, a seasoned practitioner who has trained professionals across Singapore's top institutions and corporates, our programmes go beyond theory — delivering hands-on experiences that produce measurable results in the workplace.

The rebrand from AC-Eduworks to Impactus Learning marks a shift in scope: from a founder-led consultancy to a people-first learning organisation with a vision for the future of professional development in Asia.
